About Us

 

Run by our lovely Nurse Joy and her assistants Chansey and Audino, the Pokécenter has been selling Pokémon trainers vital supplies. Specially developed in our Pokélabs, our items work on Pokémon of all sizes and levels!

In no other place can you purchase Pokéballs to capture new Pokémon friends, potions to heal injured Pokémon, both in the wild and domestically, Rawst Berries for healing burns, and Rare Candies to level up and strengthen your Pokémon. As a new addition to our wonderful shop, Nurse Joy can fully heal up to six Pokémon at a time using our state-of-the art healing station.
